BTS SIO

The BTS SIO (Computer Service for Organizations) is a Bac+2 level program, which trains people for the development and/or administration and maintenance of a computer network.

Option A SISR:
The BTS SIO SISR (System and Network Infrastructure Solution), is an option of the BTS SIO which aims to train versatile network technicians. The program provides us with the skills necessary for the management and maintenance of a company's network.

Option B SLAM:
The BTS SIO SLAM (Software Solutions and Business Applications) is a course dedicated to software development. During their two years of study, students learn to write specifications, develop applications and ensure their integration into the company's computerized systems.


Event E4

This test aims to assess the acquisition of the skills described in the “Support and provision of IT services” skills block, namely:

  • Manage IT assets.
  • Respond to incidents and requests for assistance and development.
  • Develop the organization’s online presence.
  • Work in project mode.
  • Provide users with an IT service.
  • Organize your professional development.
